Chicken curry recipe

Description

A world famous dish wit thousands of different variations.
Here we offer a very simple and quick variation that tastes as good as any other!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Dheer jeera
  • tumeric
  • Paprika
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Coconut milk
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato paste
    • Let the ancestors guide you on the right amount

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in the spices(longer the better).
  2. Brown the chicken inside a pan.
  3. Add onions and tomato and the spices to the pan.
  4. Add this mix to a blender and blend to a paste.
  5. Add chicken and mixute back to a pan.
  6. Proceed to add yogurt and/or tomato paste.
  7. Add 25ml of coconut water and a little bit of water.
  8. Leave to simmer for an hour.
  9. Salt and pepper to taste
