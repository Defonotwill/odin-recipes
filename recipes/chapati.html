Chapati recipe

Description

A staple flatbread in many homes in east africa and india that has a variety of uses that taste just as good!
This will be the east african version that has very minor changes to the original but same(if not better) taste

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1.24 cups water
  • 3 table spoons of oil(plus more for cooking and making the chapati)
  • 1 teaspoon of salt/sugar

Steps:

  1. Combine the flour oil and salt in a large bowl,be sure to add the water slowly

  2. Turn to a floured surface and knead the dough for 12-15min

  3. Leave to rest in a bowl for 30mins

  4. Turn again to a floured surface and divide into 8 equal pieces

  5. Roll out said pieces and roll them like a carpet to make them into a spiral, proceed to leave them to rest for 15minutes apply oil during this process

  6. Roll out the chapatis

  7. Add a general amount of oil into a hot pan and begin to make the chapatis

  8. Be sure to add oil when frying

  9. Serve and enjoy
