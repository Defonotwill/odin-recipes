Lamb Biryani

Description

An incredible dish originating from the indian subcontinent.The unique mix of spices as well as use of fresh mint and coriander complimented by fluffy flavoured rice will be a meal for the books.
Contains lamb meat so may not be for everyone!

Ingredients:

  • 5 Red onion
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Bundle of Coriander
  • Bundle of Mint
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 hunk of Ginger
  • 8 cloves of Garlic
  • 5 green chili
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp of tumeric powder
  • 5 tsp biryani masala
  • 5-6 star anise
  • 6 pieces of Lamb
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 8-10 cardamom pods
  • 1tbsp red chili powder
  • 10 cloves

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the lamb with the biryani,tumeric,green chili,red chili powder yogurt,half of the mint,ginger and garlic and coriander.
  2. Leave to marinate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  3. Wash the rice 30 mins before you start preparing it.
  4. Heat pan with the whole spices inside.
  5. Add the onions and cook till they are a light brown
  6. Then add the marinated lamb and let it cook on a high heat for 3 mins .
  7. drop to a low heat and let it cook for an hour and add water when needed.
  8. Place water to boil.
  9. once boiled add whole spices and salt.
  10. Once it boils again add the soaked rice inside.
  11. Let the rice cook to 90% and strain.
  12. Add the rice to the meat and garnish with mint.Leave to cook for 8 mins.
  13. Once done leave to rest for 15 mins then serve and enjoy!
